About Shiv Nadar University

Shiv Nadar University is an international, multidisciplinary, research-led university offering academic programs at various degree levels with a range of schools. The campus is erected on a sprawling expanse of 286 acres and is located in the National Capital Region. The University has achieved many milestones since its inception in 2011. In it’s maiden year, SNU had the honour of having Late Dr APJ Abdul Kalam inspiring the young minds at SNU. Back in January 2016, the University was officially inaugurated by the Hon’ble President of India- Shri Pranab Mukherjee. Dr. Raghuram Rajan Former Governor of Reserve Bank of India, Ms. Vanitha Narayan- Managing Director, IBM India Private Limited and Dr. Deepak Parekh- Chairman, HDFC Bank, Mr. Mike Lawrie, Chairman, President and CEO, DXC Technology are names of the few dignitaries that have graced SNU as chief guests for our convocation ceremonies. In addition to this, SNU is also a wonderful platform for all those who wish to make a significant difference in their stream of choice. To encourage young minds with extraordinary ideas, SNU has initiatives such as the Atal Incubation Centre, Niti Aayog.
